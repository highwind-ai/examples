This file contains the common fields for the banking dataset for the recommender model

cust_id: unique customer identifier
date_id: identifying the specific date associated with the data entry
age: represents the customer age
gender: represents if the customer is male or female
gross_income: represents the customer's gross income
owned_products: list of products owned by the customer for the latest date_id
banking_product_1: represents if the customer owns this product eg: Credit card
banking_product_2: represents if the customer owns this product eg: Savings account
banking_product_3: represents if the customer owns this product eg: Loan
banking_product_4: represents if the customer owns this product eg: Current Account
